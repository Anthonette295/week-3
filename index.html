CLAYBROOK ZOO

Welcome to Claybrook Zoo

Claybrook Zoo was founded in the 1960s by the founder Matthew Jones. It is one of the most popular zoos in the UK and the aim of this proiject is to create a software system for Claybrook Zoo. This will include a records management system (RMS), a website and also a mobile app to enhance all zoo opereations as well as streamline data management and improve the overall visitor experience.
